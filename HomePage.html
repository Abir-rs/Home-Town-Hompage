Visit Chattogram

Chattogram, Bangladesh

The sun, sand, and sea at Patenga Beach, one of Chattogram's most famous attractions

Take a refreshing swim, build sandcastles, or simply unwind with the sound of waves crashing on the shore

Immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Chattogram at the Ethnological Museum

Discover fascinating artifacts, traditional clothing, and tribal exhibits that showcase the diverse ethnic groups of the region.

Experience the tranquility of Foy's Lake with a leisurely boat ride

Surrounded by lush greenery, the lake offers a peaceful escape from the city's hustle and bustle. You can also enjoy picnics and other water activities here.
